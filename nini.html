why is it important to learn new skill ?

learning new skill can be highly beneficial to your career as it can help you
position your self to take on new project and new knowledge to advance

the importance of learning new skill are more some of them are:

it keeps you healthy,it opens doors,
it increases your adaptability and it keeps you relevant

use these steps to learn new skills

  1. set your learning goal
  2. break down your skill set
  3. identify potential barriers
  4. focus on one your skill at atime
  5. set more long term goals
  6. practice what you have learnt
  7. use mistakes as learning tool

barriers of learning new skill

  • fear of failure
  • lack of self esteem
  • lack of goals
  • poor learner experiance
  • social and cultural barriers
